About Us

"It's your turn to express and ours to address"

Echoes ensures to present you with interesting, authentic and fresh news from across the college. Covering all possible aspects of occurrence, it is a magazine run by the students, for the students.

Being The Student's Panorama of TIET, Team Echoes envisions to give you a comprehensive experience into the realms of cultural, technical, sports and academic excellence. We aim to reflect the spectrum of achievements and activities of our college societies and students, and intend to keep you updated with the college ongoings and upcomings.
